Rosebank Distillery: Located in Falkirk, Scotland, this distillery is renowned for producing the "King of the Lowlands" single malt whisky. After closing in 1993, it was reopened in 2023 following extensive restoration. The distillery now offers guided tours, tasting rooms, and opportunities to sample rare Rosebank whiskies.
