IIE Rosebank College, a brand of the Independent Institute of Education (The IIE), has recently introduced the IIE Bachelor of Laws (LLB) degree through IIE Distance (Online) Learning for the 2025 academic year. This program is designed for individuals passionate about justice and aiming to make a meaningful impact in the legal profession. Upon successful completion, graduates will be awarded the IIE Bachelor of Laws degree, conferred by The Independent Institute of Education, South Africa's leading registered private provider of higher education. Prospective students can visit the official website to learn more about admission requirements and embark on their journey to becoming legal professionals. ROSEBANKCOLLEGE.CO.ZA Additionally, the college has extended its late registration deadline until 8 March 2025 for all IIE Faculties, except for the IIE Faculty of Education, which closed on 1 March 2025. This extension provides prospective students with an additional opportunity to enroll in various programs and advance their careers.
